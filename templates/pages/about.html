What We Do

OpportunityHub is a public resource for scholarships, internships, jobs, fellowships, exchange programs, competitions and training opportunities.

Our goal is to make discovery simple: clear categories, useful filters, official application links and concise summaries that help readers decide what fits their goals.

Our Editorial Approach

We prioritize official sources, timely deadlines and practical details. Every listing should point readers toward the original application page whenever possible.
